    PM Modi in Tamil Nadu, offers prayers at Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai

    ET Online | 28 Feb 2024, 08:01 AM IST
    Prime Minister Narendra Modi on February 27 offered prayers at the Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai. Earlier in the day, he inspected an exhibition on MSME related to the automotive industry in the district. PM Modi also launched two major initiatives designed to support and uplift MSMEs in the Indian automotive industry.
